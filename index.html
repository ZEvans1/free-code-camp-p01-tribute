Animals as Leaders

Animals as Leaders members: Javier Reyes, Matt Gartska, and Tosin Abasi.

This is the paragraph about Animals as Leaders. They are: Tosin Abasi (guitar), Javier Reyes (guitar), and Matt Gartska (drums). They are usually described as a progressive metal band. A lot of their music is a combination of rock, metal, jazz, and electronic. They are also well known for using odd meters, technical riffs, and extended range guitars. They have four albums: Animals as Leaders, Weightless, Joy of Motion, and The Madness of Many.
